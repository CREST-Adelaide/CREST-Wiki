This Wiki contains references, templates, and other any other information necessary for internall collaboration and coordination among CREST members. Its content would be contributed by team members. 

## Content Guideline

**No confidential information, such as authentication credentials and intellectual properties, should be shared on this Website.** Other form of information that are not secret and are helpful for informing and collaborating with CREST members are encouraged. For instance, some types of useful content include, but not are not limited to:

- Plan and agenda for meetings
- Interesting articles to follow up on
- Technology tutorials
- Current, publicly available projects of the team
